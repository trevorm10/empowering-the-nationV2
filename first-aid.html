First Aid – R1500

Purpose: To empower learners with essential emergency response skills that can save lives in domestic and workplace settings.

Introduction: This six-month Learnership covers CPR, wound care, burn treatment, and emergency preparedness. Learners will gain hands-on experience and receive certification upon completion. Ideal for domestic workers and gardeners who want to be prepared for any situation.
